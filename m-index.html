About

Wuzopen ensures you never again have to ask yourself whether the UPS Office is open or what restaurants still serve food at 3:00AM. Wuzopen shows you what's open at this very moment, from restaurants to libraries to facilities and more.

Wuzopen was created by Kevin Lai, Tony Wu, and Francisco Tamayo for the 2012 YCC App Challenge.

How to use

White locations are open. Grey locations are closed. Search for locations by typing in the search box. Note: For irregular schedules (i.e. libraries during reading week) and holidays, the data in the application may not be accurate. The application is meant to function for the normal hours of a location. Also, Gourmet Heaven is always open.

Contact

Know a cool place that's not listed on our site? Email us at wuzopen@gmail.com, and we'll do our best to include it as soon as possible. For miscellaneous comments and sugggestions, please email wuzopen@gmail.com.
